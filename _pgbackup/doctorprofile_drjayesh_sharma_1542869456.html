Dr Jayesh Sharma

MBBS & MD

Dr Jayesh Sharma is Senior Consultant in the department of Surgical Oncology with a special interest in head and neck oncology. Dr Sharma completed his Master in Surgery from Lokmanya Tilak Medical College (Sion Hospital), Mumbai. He has subsequently trained in Surgical Oncology from Aastha Oncology Associates, Ahmedabad, and trained in Advanced Laparoscopic Surgery from Coimbatore. After his training Dr Jayesh Sharma has worked as assistant professor at PS medical College, Gujarat and 6 years at Al Sanaiya Hospital, Dubai. He has been working in Raipur since 2013. Dr Jayesh Sharma wants to introduce the best practices from India and abroad to patients at Chhattisgarh.
