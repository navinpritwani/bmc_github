Dr. Shivraj Singh Chauhan

MBBS,MS (General Surgery) | DNB (General Surgery) Fellowship in GI Surgery (AIIMS) | FICS (Gastro)

Dr Chauhan is an accomplished Surgical Specialist with experience in Gen Surgery, Minimal Access Surgery, Lap GI Oncology, GI Surgery and Consulting; formerly worked as Dean-Academics & HOD-Surgical Gastroenterology at Command Hospital Western Command, Panchkula. He has extensive knowledge of Surgical Gastroenterology with hands-on experience in performing almost all GI Surgical Operations including Lap GI Oncology and other laparoscopic procedures including Bariatric Surgery with a fair degree of competence. He also possesses a vast teaching experience having worked as Professor of Surgery at Armed Forces Medical College Pune , Army College of Medical Sciences New Delhi and Associate Prof GI Surgery Army Hospital Research & Referral New Delhi across India; skilled in leading (MBBS),Post Graduate (MS), DNB (Surgery), DNB (GI Surgery)students and monitoring their performance & progress.
