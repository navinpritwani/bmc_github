BMC recruitment disclaimer and notification

BALCO Medical Centre has been informed, that in the recent past some individuals and recruitment agencies have tried to mislead the public by fictitiously claiming to be employees of BALCO Medical Centre or posing as authorized recruitment agencies of BALCO Medical Centre. We at BALCO Medical Centre, follow a formal meritocratic recruitment process through our HR department and do not ask for any security deposit or fees (refundable or non-refundable) at any stage of the recruitment process. We, therefore, appeal to the public to not respond to any such emails or job offers.

How to protect yourself from a recruitment fraud? Here are a few steps to follow:

The golden rules:

1. Any job offer that requires you to pay a fee in advance or for a completed process is probably a scam. No money transfers, payments or security deposits of any kind will ever be requested from applicants by BALCO Medical Centre or any of the agencies that recruit on our behalf, at any point in the recruitment process.

2. Legitimate job-related emails will come from corporate email accounts with the domain, e.g. @vedanta.co.in. Any recruiter or company that corresponds from a free email account such as Yahoo, Live, Hotmail or Gmail is very likely a scammer. Sometimes scammers also use software to generate fake email ids and fake company letterheads to make it appear genuine, and hence you must exercise caution for any job offers you receive.

3. Scammers often use fake URLs to mask themselves as large, well-known corporates. Double-check the URL or the web address of the company.

4. Please note that BALCO Medical Centre shall not be liable for any loss or damage that may be suffered or incurred directly or indirectly through correspondence with fraudulent individuals or recruitment agencies and that such communication should not be treated as an offer or representation from BALCO Medical Centre.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at the ‘contact us’ link available on our website.

Surgical Oncology

The only subspecialty based Surgical Oncology service in the region with dedicated Organ based specialists, working on fully integrated Operation Theatres. The hospital has 5 dedicated major Operation theatres for all kinds of cancer surgeries, and brachy therapy.

Radiation Oncology

This is a complete radiation treatment centre with the first True Beam Linear Accelerator & the first 24 Channel Brachy Therapy of Chhattisgarh. We also offer Low Dose as well as High Dose Radioiodine Treatment, making this the most complete treatment centre.

Medical Oncology

We offer a dedicated 20 bed day care centre with dedicated area for paediatric patients, making this the largest day care unit of the state. We treat all kinds of solid and haematological malignancies using Chemotherapy, targeted therapy, Immunotherapy and other modalities. As per the international guidelines, chemo mixing is done in a separate area using fume hood, and delivered by dedicated trained oncology nurses.

Pain and Palliative Care

The first hospital with many trained pain specialists as well as a palliative care specialist ensure that at every stage of life and disease a cancer patient will be comfortable and symptom free.

Laboratory Services

Super-speciality services with Haematology, Clinical Pathology, Histopathology, Cytopathology, Clinical biochemist, Microbiology and Infection control departments and services such as Immunohistochemistry, Flow Cytometry in addition to all routine investigations by completely automated machines.

Radiology Services

The only Siemens Amira MRI in the region with high end 64 Slice Siemens CT scan, Digital Mammography, Digital X-ray, High end GE Ultrasound machines, all integrated with PACS making it a film and paperless department. In addition the department of Nuclear medicine offers the first integrated package of PET CT & SPECT CT in state. This creates the best package for early and detailed imaging of all kinds of cancer.

Intensive Care

Critical Care Unit features a 36 bed ICU divided in Medical,, Surgical and HDU units with 2 isolation beds with separate ventilation for immunocompromised patients. This is manned by a round the clock coverage of Intensivists and qualified residents. This is the largest dedicated Oncology ICU service in the region.

Medical Director

Dr Rakesh Kumar Vyas is a seasoned oncologist and medical administrator with over 30 years of experience in teaching and patient care. Dr Vyas has sound academic credentials as well. He has had multiple Fellowship stints overseas. Dr Vyas is also concurrently the Chairman of the BA Cancer Centre, Navsari, besides being involved in the establishment of a cancer centre in Sidhpur and a project in Rajkot, which is in the pipeline. He has also travelled extensively to further advance his clinical experience and get exposure to the best practices from all over the world.
