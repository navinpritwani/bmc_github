Medical Camps 

Recognising the prime importance of early detection in effective treatment of cancer, BMC is also focusing on detecting the disease as early as possible. Keeping this in mind, we have now started conducting Medical Camps across the local regions surrounding Raipur.

Our first Free Cancer Screening Camp was held at Nawapara-Rajim, on the banks of the mighty Mahanadi River on 29th July 2018. A couple of days earlier, on the occasion of Head & Neck Cancer day, another camp was held at UltraTech RMC’s factory in Mandir Hasaud. This is just the start of the incredible journey BMC has embarked on, a journey that will one day make BMC the preferred oncology centre in the region.​
